Runari OS: the operating system behind the growth.

Most businesses run marketing across six disconnected tools — and leads fall through every gap between them. Runari OS unifies your leads, conversations, appointments, automations, reviews, and reporting into one powerful platform.

Everything in one place

CRM & Pipeline

Every lead, call, and conversation in one view. Drag deals through stages, see exactly where every job stands, and never lose track of a prospect again.

Online Booking

Let customers book estimates and service calls straight from your website, ads, or a text message — synced to your team's real calendar.

Missed-Call Text-Back

62% of calls to small businesses go unanswered. Runari OS texts every missed caller back within seconds, so the lead talks to you instead of your competitor.

Email & SMS Follow-Up

Automated sequences that nurture every lead — quotes that follow up on themselves, reminders that cut no-shows, and reactivation campaigns for past customers.

Funnels & Landing Pages

High-converting pages for every campaign, built in the same platform that captures and routes the leads they generate.

Review Automation

Automatically request Google reviews after every completed job, respond from one inbox, and watch your local rankings climb with your rating.

Leads, calls, bookings, revenue, and ROAS — live, in plain English, on one screen. Know what every dollar is doing.

AI-Assisted Tools

AI-drafted replies, conversation summaries, and smart routing that answer leads fast — even after hours.
