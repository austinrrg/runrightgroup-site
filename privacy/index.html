Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

The Run Right Group ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect through runrightgroup.com, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Information you provide: name, email address, phone number, business name, and any details you share in our forms or during consultations.
  • Information collected automatically: IP address, browser type, pages visited, and similar analytics data collected via cookies and comparable technologies.

How we use your information

  • To respond to inquiries and schedule consultations you request.
  • To provide, improve, and market our services.
  • To send transactional messages related to your inquiry, including by SMS where you have consented.

SMS / text messaging

If you provide your phone number and consent, we may send text messages related to your inquiry. Message frequency may vary; message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for help. Consent to receive texts is not a condition of purchase. We do not share SMS consent or phone numbers obtained for SMS purposes with third parties for their marketing.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with service providers that help us operate our website, CRM, and communications (under confidentiality obligations), or when required by law.

Cookies & analytics

We use cookies and analytics tools to understand site usage and improve performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data retention & security

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purposes above and protect it with reasonable administrative and technical safeguards.
